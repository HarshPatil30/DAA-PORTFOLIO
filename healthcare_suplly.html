Hospital Supply Chain Management

Introduction

Effective management of the hospital supply chain ensures that critical medical supplies are always available to departments and healthcare providers. The challenge lies in optimizing procurement, storage, and distribution to maintain efficiency and reduce costs.

Different modules and their implementation

storage and Order Management

The problem is optimized using a heap (priority queue) to prioritize medical supplies based on urgency, ensuring minimal storage costs while maintaining the integrity of the products through efficient inventory management

Distribution Routing

Implement graph algorithms such as Dijkstra's to optimize delivery routes from storage areas to hospital departments, ensuring that medical supplies are delivered efficiently and on time, reducing delivery costs.

Supplier Selection and Evaluation

Design a decision-support model that helps hospital managers evaluate and select suppliers based on criteria such as cost, delivery time, and reliability, ensuring that hospitals work with the most reliable suppliersusing a greedy algorithm.

Inventory management

Manage medicine and stock inventory using min-heap, sorting for optimizing stock items and discarding expired items
